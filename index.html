Our Scout Groups

Birchanger Headquarters

Our District Headquarters sits in the quiet village of Birchanger on the western edge of Hatfield Forest District, bordering Bishop's Stortford. Whilst there is currently no active scout group meeting at the hall, it is actively used by the support team for training and events, and the facilities there are available for wider use.
